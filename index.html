The Story

Built by a bread lover, for bread lovers

Dough started with a simple frustration — I kept walking past the same chain coffee shops while amazing independent bakeries were tucked around the corner, totally undiscovered.

I'm Stefan, and I've been obsessed with good bread my whole life. There's something about the craft of it — the time, the skill, the ingredients — that deserves to be celebrated. Good bread made well is one of life's genuine pleasures, and the people making it deserve more than to be overshadowed by the big names.

So I built Dough. A place to find those bakeries, support them, and help more people discover what great bread actually tastes like.

Frequently Asked Questions
Open the menu (top left) and tap Suggest a Bakery. Fill in as much detail as you can — name, address, website and Instagram all help us verify and add it faster. We review every suggestion and aim to add new bakeries regularly.
On any bakery's detail page, scroll down and tap Report an Error. Tell us what's wrong — opening hours, address, or anything else — and we'll update it as soon as possible.
Tap the heart icon on any bakery's detail page. Your saved bakeries are stored on your device and accessible from the Favourites section in the menu.
In the Favourites section, tap New List to create a custom collection — great for organising by neighbourhood, occasion, or anything else. You can add any bakery to a list from its detail page.
Dough currently covers London, with more cities coming soon. If you'd like to see your city added, drop us an email — knowing where demand is helps us prioritise.
Dough collects approximate location data and anonymous usage data (such as which bakeries are viewed and which filters are used) to improve the app. We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information. No data is sold or shared with advertisers.
The featured bakery rotates weekly and is chosen automatically — it cycles through all bakeries in your city before repeating any. It's not paid placement; every bakery gets a turn.
